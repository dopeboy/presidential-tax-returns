Presidential Tax Returns: Income, Tax Rates, & Charity Rates

Since the early 1970s, presidents and presidental candidates have voluntarily released their tax returns in order to provide transparency. Tax returns help answer questions like:

How much do they pay in taxes? Do they give to charity? Do they use tax loopholes? Do they keep money offshore? Are they financially beholden to other people or entities?

We hope the data below will give you insight into these questions across past presidents and candidates so that you can better evaluate current and future candidates.

Presidents talk a lot of about taxes. Let's find out how much they actually pay.

Since the early 1970s, presidents and presidental candidates have voluntarily released their tax returns in order to provide transparency. Tax returns help answer questions like:

Do you pay taxes? Do you give to charity? Do you use tax loopholes? Do you keep your money offshore? Can you properly manage your family's finances? Are you financially beholden to other people or entities?

We hope the data elow will give you insight into these questions across past presidents and candidates so that you can better evaluate current and future candidates.
